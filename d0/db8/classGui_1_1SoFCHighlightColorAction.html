The order of the defined SO_ACTION_ADD_METHOD statements is very important. First the base classes and afterwards subclasses of them must be listed, otherwise the registered methods of subclasses will be overridden. For more details see the thread in the Coin3d forum https://www.coin3d.org/pipermail/coin-discuss/2004-May/004346.html. This means that SoSwitch must be listed after SoGroup and SoFCSelection after SoSeparator because both classes inherits the others.
