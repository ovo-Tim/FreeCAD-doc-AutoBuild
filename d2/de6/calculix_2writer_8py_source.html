73 units_information = """***********************************************************
74 ** About units:
75 ** See ccx manual, ccx does not know about any unit.
76 ** Golden rule: The user must make sure that the numbers they provide have consistent units.
77 ** The user is the FreeCAD calculix writer module ;-)
78 **
79 ** The unit system which is used at Guido Dhondt's company: mm, N, s, K
80 ** Since Length and Mass are connected by Force, if Length is mm the Mass is in t to get N
81 ** The following units are used to write to inp file:
82 **
83 ** Length: mm (this includes the mesh geometry)
84 ** Mass: t
85 ** TimeSpan: s
86 ** Temperature: K
87 **
88 ** This leads to:
89 ** Force: N
90 ** Pressure: N/mm^2 == MPa (Young's Modulus has unit Pressure)
91 ** Density: t/mm^3
92 ** Gravity: mm/s^2
93 ** Thermal conductivity: t*mm/K/s^3 == as W/m/K == kW/mm/K
94 ** Specific Heat: mm^2/s^2/K = J/kg/K == kJ/t/K
95 """
