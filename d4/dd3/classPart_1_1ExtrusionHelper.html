checkInnerWires: Checks what wires are inner ones by taking a set of prisms created with every wire. The prisms are cut from each other. If the moment of inertia thereby changes, the prism wire is an inner wire. Inner wires can have nested inner wires that are then in fact outer wires. Therefore checkInnerWires is called recursively until all wires are checked.
