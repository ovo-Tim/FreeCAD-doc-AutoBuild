54 /* This class is used to manage the overriding of mouse pointer coordinates in Sketcher
55  * (in Edit-Mode) depending on the situation. Those situations are in priority order :
56  * 1 - Snap at angle: For tools like Slot, Arc, Line, Ellipse, this enables to constrain the angle
57  * at steps of 5° (or customized angle). This is useful to make features at a certain angle (45° for
58  * example) 2 - Snap to object: This snaps the mouse pointer onto objects. 3 - Snap to grid: This
59  * snaps the mouse pointer on the grid.
60  */
