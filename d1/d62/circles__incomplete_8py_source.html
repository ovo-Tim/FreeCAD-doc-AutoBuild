24 """Provides various incomplete functions for creating circles.
25 
26 These functions cannot be used because they require that certain functions
27 to calculate shapes exist first.
28 
29 These functions are meant to create circles starting from three values,
30 either a tangent edge, a point, or a radius. Various combinations
31 are possible.
32 
33 - Two tangents and a point.
34 - Two tangents and a radius.
35 - A tangent and two points.
36 - A tangent, a point, and a radius.
37 - Three tangents.
38 
39 In this case, the word 'tangent' refers to either a straight line
40 or to a circular edge, so even more combinations are possible.
41 
42 - Two tangents and a point.
43  * Line, line, and point.
44  * Circle, line, and point.
45  * Line, circle, and point.
46  * Circle, circle, and point.
47 
48 And so on, with the other combinations.
49 """
