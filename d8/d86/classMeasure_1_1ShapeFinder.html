traverse the tree from leafSub up to rootObject, obtaining placements along the way. Note that the placements will need to be applied in the reverse order (ie top down) of what is delivered in plm stack. leafSub is a dot separated longSubName which DOES NOT include rootObject. the result does not include rootObject's transform.
