◆ DoInitialParameterCorrection()

bool ParameterCorrection::DoInitialParameterCorrection ( double  fSizeFactor = 0.0f)
protectedvirtual

Calculates an initial area at the beginning of the algorithm. For this purpose, the best-fit plane for the point cloud is calculated. The points are calculated with respect to the base consisting of the eigenvectors of the covariance matrix and projected onto the best-fit plane. The bounding box is calculated from these points, then the u/v parameters for the points are calculated.
