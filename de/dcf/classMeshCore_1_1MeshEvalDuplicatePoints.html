Detailed Description

The MeshEvalDuplicatePoints class searches for duplicated points. A point is regarded as duplicated if the distances between x, y and z coordinates of two points is less than an epsilon (defined by MeshDefinitions::_fMinPointDistanceD1, default value=1.0e-5f).
