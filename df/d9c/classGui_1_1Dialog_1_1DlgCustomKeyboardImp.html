Detailed Description

Shows an overview of all available commands of all groups and modules. You can customize your workbenches just by drag&dropping any commands onto the toolbars or commandbars. But you cannot modify any standard toolbars or commandbars such as "File, View, ...". It is only possible to customize your own toolbars or commandbars.

Author
Werner Mayer
